Many bacterial species in the world still remain unknown, and Roza, our biological researcher, has jumped into the quest to find bacteria that has the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Her lab partnered with Improbable Beef, a startup, in hopes to find the bacteria within our human body, more specifially in belly button, that will provide the perfect beef taste. To provide a little background, our body serves as a source of thousands of bacteria, and different species of bacteria harbor different parts of our body. Also, across different people, different bacteria may harbor the same body part, say, a belly button. Roza and her team has collected bacterial samples from people across the country and identified the bacteria and their number in the first steps to find the bacteria.
